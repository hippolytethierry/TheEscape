THE ESCAPIST 
Created By Hippolyte Thierry in 2021

WINDOWS

The .exe is located at TheEscapist\Builds\Windows\Maquette.exe

MAC OS

The game is located at TheEscapist\Builds\MAC_OS.app\Contents\MacOS\Maquette

PLAY LOCAL

First add an ip rule to your home server port 7777 to the machine hosting the game (optional)

Launch the lobby by chosing "Local" button then 3 others players can join.

Press the "Start Game" button. It'll launch TheEscapist.

HAVE FUN !
